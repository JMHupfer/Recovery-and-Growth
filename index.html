About Me

Hello! I’m a 2019 graduate with a degree in International Business, and I’m returning to a life that values recovery, reflection, writing, and meaningful growth. I’m still building as a developer, but my focus is broader now: creating with intention, taking care of myself, and learning how to live and work in a way that feels honest and sustainable. I completed a full-stack bootcamp in 2023, and I’m continuing to grow through writing and the ongoing work of becoming more grounded and capable. Outside of technology, I’m drawn to travel, culture, and the quiet discipline of self-development. My goal is to show myself that recovery can be fun and to track my progress while still staying true to who I am, where I was, and where I am heading. The goal is one day at a time.
